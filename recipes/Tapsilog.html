Tapsilog

Description

A very local and famous go-to meal by the Filipino people that is very beefy
and juicy and has the familiar taste of home especially when partered with it's sauce.

The reason why it's called Tapsilog is because of it's components which are tapa, sinangag and itlog. (beef, garlic rice, egg)

Ingredients

  • Beef Loin
  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Side Dish

Steps on how to make this meal

  1. Frst, you need to cook the beef
  2. Second, you need to cook the egg (Sunny side up or Omellete)
  3. Third, you need to cook the garlic rice (Sinangag)
