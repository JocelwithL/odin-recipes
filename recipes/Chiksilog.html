Chiksilog

Description

A very local and famous go-to meal by the Filipino people that is very tender
and juicy and has the familiar taste of home especially when partered with it's sauce.

The reason why it's called Chiksilog is because of it's components which are Chicken, sinangag and itlog. (beef, garlic rice, egg)

Ingredients

  • Chicken part
  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Side Dish

Steps on how to make this meal

  1. Frst, you need to fry the chicken
  2. Second, you need to cook the egg (Sunny side up or Omellete)
  3. Third, you need to cook the garlic rice (Sinangag)
