Porksilog

Description

A very local and famous go-to meal by the Filipino people that is very porky
and juicy and has the familiar taste of home especially when partered with it's sauce.

The reason why it's called Porksilog is because of it's components which are pork, sinangag and itlog. (beef, garlic rice, egg)

Ingredients

  • Pork Loin
  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Side Dish

Steps on how to make this meal

  1. Frst, you need to cook the pork
  2. Second, you need to cook the egg (Sunny side up or Omellete)
  3. Third, you need to cook the garlic rice (Sinangag)
